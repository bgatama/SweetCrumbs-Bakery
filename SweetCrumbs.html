ℹ️About us

Founded in 2010 by Grace & Samuel Kariuki with a single oven and dream to bring artisan baking to Nairobi.

Over the years, we have grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery trusted by thousands of families, offices, and event planners across the city. Our bakers rise before dawn every single day to ensure that what lands on your table is always fresh, always flavourful, and always made with care.

We believe baking is an art, not a factory process. That is why we never use artificial presertatives, and we source our flour, eggs, and dairy from certified local farms within 100km of Nairobi
