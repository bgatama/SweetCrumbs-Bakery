About Us

Founded in 2010 by Grace & Samuel Kariuki with one small oven and a dream to bring artisan baking to Nairobi.

Today SweetCrumbs Bakery serves thousands of happy customers every month. Every morning our bakers begin before sunrise to ensure every loaf and pastry reaches you fresh.

Our mission is simple: Bake fresh. Serve with love. Support local farmers.
